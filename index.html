Policies

Lash Refills

I do not offer foreign fills. Clients will be required to book a removal and full set in this case. Clients must have 40% of their lashes to be considered a fill. Clients must have 85% of their lashes to be considered for a mini fill (within 7 days). Sets after the 3 week mark will be charged as a full set.

Nail Refills

I do not offer foreign fills. clients will be required to book a removal and full set in this case. Clients must notify me whether they want a colour change or a design change. please note that a colour change and a design change are not the same! Design changes will cost more than a colour change. Also notify me of any nails that need to be replaced. Replacing nails will be an additional charge based on products used, length of nail, ETC.

Grace Period

All clients will recieve a 10 minute grace period. Clients are required to update me if they will be late. Arrival past the 10 minute grace period will result in a $10 late fee added to the service. Arrival past 20 minutes of the appointment may result in cancellation if i am unableto extent the appointment. If i am able to accomodate past 20 nminutes there will be an additional $10 late fee added to the service for a total of $20.

Rescheduling and Cancellation

All clients are required to provide me with at least 24-hours notice for cancelling or rescheduling appointments. Deposits are valid for a single reschedule date within 30 days of the original appoinment date. Clients rescheduling without a 24-hour notice will be required to re-book with a new deposit. no shows will be banned from booking again in the future. Please respect my time.
